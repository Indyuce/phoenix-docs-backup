# 🏠 Home

***
**Welcome to the MythicLib documentation!**
***

MythicLib is a powerful library designed to guarantee that all MMO plugins are both standalone and 100% compatible with each other. It provides a wide range of features and utilities that greatly simplify the development process for MMO plugins.

::: tip
MythicLib is available for free on [SpigotMC](https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/mmolib-mythiclib.90306)
:::

If something is not quite clear, please join our [Discord](https://phoenixdevt.fr/discord) server to get help from staff or community! We do our best to keep this wiki up to date. Let us know if you see a typo or anything outdated!
