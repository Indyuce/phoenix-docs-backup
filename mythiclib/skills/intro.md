# ☄️ Introduction

Skills are everywhere in MMOCore or MMOItems. They can be bound to items or player classes or even be temporarily granted to players when wearing a full set of armor. Skills can do anything from damaging your enemies to buffing your team mates; they can be active (a firebolt that casts on right clicks) or passive (extra damage when the player is on fire).

::: info
Since MythicLib 1.3, skills from both MMOItems and MMOCore are centralized into MythicLib. Therefore you can use MMOItems abilities in MMOCore player classes, and bind MMOCore skills to MMOItems items/item sets.
:::

Skills have numerical **parameters** (damage, cooldown, duration...) that you can customize to adapt your skill power to your item or player level. For instance, you may edit how much damage an offensive skill does, how long some potion effect lasts, how much mana the skill consumes...

## Custom skills

You can register custom skills inside of all MMO plugins using popular plugins featuring a custom scripting language:

- [MythicMobs](custom/mythic.md)
- [MythicLib](custom/mythiclib.md)
- [Fabled](custom/fabled.md)
- [CoreTools](custom/coretools.md)
