# 😉 When Logging In

When a player logs in, they become invincible and are forced to select a profile in the Profiles GUI (see next page). Once they have chosen their profile, all the data from that profile will be loaded and applied to the player. The player can create or remove profiles in the GUI, but they are limited to a certain amount of simultaneous profiles. This can be fully customized using the rank system (more here).

Here is an extensive list of all the vanilla features that are saved and applied between profiles:
- Health, Food level and saturation
- Air Level
- Level & Experience
- Last Location & Bed Spawn Location
- Inventory, Equipment & Ender chest
- Gamemode
- Potion Effects
- Vanilla Attributes
