Custom Mechanics
----------
Custom mechanics for the MythicMobs plugin to make extra-special ability mechanics!

| Mechanic               | Description                                                    |
|------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|
| [Arrow Volley](MythicMob-Mechanics/MMOItems-Arrow-Volley)   | Fire arrows at the target location, arrows scale with caster's stats. |
| [OnUse Aura](MythicMob-Mechanics/On-MMOItem-Use-Aura)   | Aura that triggers with special weapon attacks (Lutes/Staff/Musket...) |


Drop MMOItems from MythicMobs
----------
You can add items from MI to MythicMobs drop tables: more info on [this page](Item%20Drop%20Tables#adding-mmoitems-to-mythicmobs-drop-tables).
